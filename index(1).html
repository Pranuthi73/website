Hindustani classical music is the foundation of any form of instrumental music or singing in any language from the northern regions of the Indian subcontinent Hindi, Urdu, Punjabi, Gujarati, Marathi, Bengali, Pashto, Nepali, and others, whether it is Film Songs, Bhajans, Ghazals or Folk Songs.
If you want to sing well, there is simply no substitute to methodically learning the basics of Sargam, Alankar, Raag, Aalaap, Taal, and Taan.
Hindustani classical music has a long tradition of being taught and learnt face-to-face. These free online lessons are our humble contribution to help those who wish to learn this sublime, soulful music, but do not have access to a good teacher locally from whom they can learn face-to-face.
